Our approach blends technology and strategy to deliver solutions that are not only technically robust but also closely aligned with the client’s business goals and long-term vision.

We are officially incorporated as a Private Limited Company under the Companies Act, 2013, recognized by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India. This legal structure gives us the credibility and operational capacity to engage in long-term contracts, scale operations across geographies, and offer equity partnerships to investors. Being a Pvt. Ltd. company enhances our brand trust among clients, vendors, and institutions, and allows us to operate with the governance standards expected of a registered Indian corporate entity.

TimesPoint Technologies is also proudly registered under the Government of India’s MSME (Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises) program. This registration validates our compliance with the norms of ethical business practices, quality service delivery, and transparent operations. As an MSME, we are eligible for government benefits such as funding support, procurement preference, and access to subsidies, all of which further strengthen our ability to deliver cost-effective, high-impact digital solutions to clients nationwide.

Adding to our credentials, we are officially recognized by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) under the Startup India initiative. This distinction highlights our contribution to solving real-world problems through innovation and scalable technology. Our DPIIT recognition provides us with access to startup benefits like tax exemptions, funding opportunities, and participation in government-led innovation challenges. Moreover, our listing among Bhaskar Startups acknowledges our active role in regional entrepreneurship and our commitment to India’s mission of self-reliance, digital empowerment, and youth-led innovation.

Times Point Technologies is also officially registered with Dun & Bradstreet under the D-U-N-S® (Data Universal Numbering System), a globally recognized business identification system used by corporations, governments, and institutions worldwide. This registration reinforces our business credibility, making us more accessible for international partnerships, global procurement opportunities, and enterprise collaborations. Being D-U-N-S® registered enables us to maintain transparency, trust, and traceability in financial and commercial dealings—further enhancing our reputation as a dependable technology partner in both domestic and global markets.
